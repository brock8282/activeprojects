How to Add Your Email Signature in Microsoft Outlook

Print These Instructions

Click on your name in the directory on the left

This will open a new window displaying only your email signature

Select all (CTRL, A for Windows) (Command A for Apple)

Right click and select copy

From within Outlook, click on File (top left of the screen)

From the menu bar on the left, select Options

From the new window Outlook Options, select Mail (top left of the window)

Then select the Signaturesâ€¦ button (right side of window)

Create a name for your signature (Your Name)

After you have typed a name for your signature, select it.

Paste the contents from step 3 (CTRL, V for Windows) (Command V for Apple)

After you have pasted, select the signature for both New and Replies and click OK
